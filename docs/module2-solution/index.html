Culitivator

A cultivator is any of several types of farm implement used for secondary tillage. One sense of the name refers to frames with teeth (also called shanks) that pierce the soil as they are dragged through it linearly.

Cage-wheel

Rice is a main crop in India. ... To mulch the soil properly and mix the same in wet condition, full cage wheel is used. Especially in the are where deep water puddling is done, full cage wheels are used.

Straw Baler

A baler or hay baler is a piece of farm machinery used to compress a cut and raked crop (such as hay, cotton, flax straw, salt marsh hay, or silage) into compact bales that are easy to handle, transport, and store.
